NovaBank: online banking app

A full online bank. Register and you get a current account, savings account, and debit card. Transfer money by account number, freeze your card, filter your statement and export it to CSV, and watch a monthly spending chart. Admins get their own panel with customer search, account freezing, and an audit log. Built with TypeScript, PostgreSQL, and Prisma: transfers run inside a database transaction so money can't go missing halfway.

Next.js TypeScript Express Prisma PostgreSQL Tailwind CSS

LuckyReels: online casino

An online casino where the browser only animates: slots, hi-lo, and the lucky wheel outcomes are decided on the server. Accounts with bcrypt and httpOnly JWT cookies, login lockout after five failed attempts, an 18+ age check, a cashier with Luhn-validated cards, withdrawals, a transaction ledger, and daily deposit limits. Play money only.
